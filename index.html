The Invitation

This is not a retreat where you sit still. Re-naturalize Your Mind moves you into the soil, the compost pile, and the medicine garden, in the quiet hours before the Yucatán heat sets in. Over six days you will build what regenerates long after you leave: a garden, a practice, and a rhythm of rest that belongs to you.

The Rhythm of Your Days

Mornings Move With the Land

Every day begins at 6 AM in the garden, hands in the dirt before the sun climbs too high. Breakfast follows between 7 and 9 AM, taken slow, taken together. Afternoons rest. Evenings hold space for reflection, ceremony, and workshop sessions under open sky.

The Work of the Week

What You'll Build

Composting

Learn to turn what's discarded into what feeds. A hands on practice in building living soil from the ground up.

Permaculture

Study the patterns that let land sustain itself, and carry those same principles back to how you sustain you.

Holistic Health

Root level healing, movement, and medicine, guided by practitioners who understand the whole woman.
