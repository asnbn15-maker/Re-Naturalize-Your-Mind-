The Invitation

This is not a retreat where you sit still. Re-naturalize Your Mind moves you into the soil, the compost pile, and the medicine garden, in the quiet hours before the Yucatán heat sets in. Join for Part A, Part B, or the Full Retreat, and build what regenerates long after you leave: a garden, a practice, and a living classroom that grows for years to come.

The Garden We're Building

Rooted at the Center

A living ankh at the heart of the garden. Life, growth, and rest rooted in the same soil.
